CalorieLens — Privacy Policy

Effective: March 23, 2026

Overview

CalorieLens ("the App") is developed by MossCorp. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Data Stored On Your Device

  • Meal entries: Food names, calorie and macro data, timestamps, and photos you capture are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • Settings: Daily calorie goals, onboarding state, and notification preferences are stored in UserDefaults on your device.

Data Sent to Third Parties

  • Food recognition: When you scan a food photo, the image may be sent to OpenAI's API (GPT-4o-mini) for nutritional analysis. OpenAI processes the image to identify the food and return calorie/macro estimates. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this processing: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy. When offline, recognition runs entirely on-device.
  • Subscriptions: In-app purchases are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. RevenueCat collects anonymous purchase data to manage your subscription. RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Apple HealthKit: If you grant permission, the App reads and writes calorie and nutrition data to Apple Health. This data stays on your device and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We never access your HealthKit data on any server.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require an account or login.
  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or advertising trackers.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

All meal data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, all local data is removed. Food images sent to OpenAI for recognition are processed transiently and are not stored by MossCorp.

Your Choices

  • You can deny camera and photo library access — the App offers manual food entry.
  • You can deny HealthKit access — the App works fully without it.
  • You can delete all data from Settings within the App.
  • You can use the App offline — food recognition falls back to on-device processing.

Children's Privacy

CalorieLens is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
