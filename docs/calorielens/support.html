Common Questions

How does food scanning work?

Point your camera at any food and tap the capture button. CalorieLens uses AI to identify the food and estimate calories and macros. For best results, photograph individual items or simple plates.

How accurate are the results?

CalorieLens provides estimates based on AI analysis. Results are approximate and intended for general tracking, not medical or clinical use. You can manually correct any result.

Can I use it offline?

Yes. CalorieLens has on-device food recognition that works without an internet connection. Online mode provides more accurate results for unusual foods.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will restore automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel.
